FAQ

Quick answers for calculator users.

Does this calculator guarantee results?

No. It is a simplified projection tool and should be used for rough planning, not as a promise of actual investment performance.

Why is annual return adjustable?

Different assets, strategies, and time periods perform differently. A flexible assumption is more useful than pretending one fixed rate is always correct.
